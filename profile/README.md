
# van der Weide Lab
Chromatin Systems Biology

Our lab investigates the fundamental regulatory logic of the genome. We believe that understanding cellular health and disease requires connecting the dots across multiple scales: from the physical structure and modifications of chromatin to gene expression.

Part of the [Center for Molecular Medicine](https://www.cmmutrecht.eu), Utrecht, The Netherlands
